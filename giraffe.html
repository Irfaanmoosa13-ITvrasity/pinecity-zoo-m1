GIRAFFE

Giraffes are the worlds tallest mammals. The long legs allow the giraffes to run as fast as 35 miles an hour over short distance and comfortably cruise at 10 miles an hour over longer distances. Giraffes have an average lifespan of about 25 years iin their natural habitat and about 20 to 27 years in human care. Giraffes eat leaves and twigs of acacia, mimosa and wild apricot trees.
