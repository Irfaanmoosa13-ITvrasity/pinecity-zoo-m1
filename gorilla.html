ANIMALS MAP PLACES
GORILLA

Gorillas have large humal-like hands with muscular arms that are longer than their legs. Gorillas love to wrestle. Just like human children, gorillas will sometimes wrestle with each other during bouts of play. Gorillas live for about 35-40 years. Gorillas stick to a vegetarian diet feeding on stems, bamboo shoots and fruits.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the Gorillas next to Monekt Trail.
